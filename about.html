About Casa Vesela

Located in a quiet part of the village, between rolling hills and at the edge of the forest, Casa Vesela guesthouse offers its visitors an authentic Romanian countryside experience.

The common areas include indoor and outdoor dining areas, a cosy lounge with TV & a small but well catered library with books and magazines in several languages. The patio is an excellent place to dine, or relax & soak out the sun in warm days. The big yard can be a safe playground for kids and adults alike.

For our guests who would like to experience the full country-side experience, we are always happy to provide varied typical activities, around the farm. You are welcome to join us tend to the animals, care for our gardens & vineyards or experience cooking Romanian traditional recipes!

The more active of our guests can enjoy guided or independent bike rides, horse rides, and trecks. Picnics can also be organized upon request.

Accomodation

Our four different rooms are comfortably furnished & equipped, with individual bathrooms. Each room has its unique character and charm.

Location and Directions

You can find us in the South of Romania, at 5 Sunshine and Smiles street, village Always Happy.

    Distance from major cities:
  • Bucharest: 100km
  • Pitesti: 20km
  • Curtea de Arges: 60km

By train: The closest train station from our village is located 10km away, in the nearby village of "Heaven's Edge". From there, we can arrange a car transfer to the guesthouse, based on your needs. In the winter, we offer our most adventurous customers also transfer by horse drawn sleigh.

By car: National road DN250 passes by our village at km 55. The entrance to the village is marked by a road sign. Once you see the sign, turn in the indicated direction and you'll be 3 straigh kilometers away from our guesthouse.

By bus: The nearby city of Pitesti is connected by bus with many cities in Romania. From there, you can either rent a car and follow the directions indicated above, or we are happy to organize a transfer for you.

We also offer our clients transfer from the international airport Henry Coanda. Please contact us for further details or clarifications.
